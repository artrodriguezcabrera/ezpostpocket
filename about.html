About us

Our Story

Vinylast Inc. is a family owned business which started in 1974. We have been innovating, designing and manufacturing products in the decking and railing industries for over 25 years. We offer a variety of patented products including ADA hand rail and CCRR deck post mounts. Our ADA components include die cast, extruded and stamped parts which support many types of decking and railing systems.

Vinylast, working together with our customers, supply not only exceptional products but we offer solutions and designs making our products better, easier to install, stronger and above all safer.

Making products that solve problems is what we do best.

Vinylast works with accredited testing facilities to ensure that our products are made of the best quality and are manufactured to the most recent building codes available.
